EMPRESS AVENUE

Empress Avenue Media allowed me to be part of the social media campaign for the release of Penny Girl, an amazing short film about a young trans man living with a rare genetic disorder embraces his new identity. This is a project I hold dear to me and I'm very thankful that I was able to be part of this short film's journey as part of the social media creation team.
