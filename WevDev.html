Colab Peru

I create a website for a project for SFU Graduate Beedie School called CoLab Peru. Previously they only had a blog where as now they needed a brand new website where they could store information that can be used for conferences and the people involved with them as well as anyone else who wanted to help or stay updated with their work. The website also has information about how to get involved. This project came to be very important to me as I learned on how to work with non-developers, seeing their needs and creating solutions for them. I believe the project was a success as the website is still being used to this day. Due to the project being finished, the website is not quite available but thanks to Wayback Machine there is a snipped saved.
